Let's explore the bandarban

Bandarban is the name of a hill girl full of natural beauty. As far as the eye can see the greenery around.If you want to touch the clouds with your hands or if you want to be immersed in the beauty of the mountains, you can come back with your family or friends from this unique paradise of nature. Famous sights There are Nilachal, Nilgiri, Golden Temple, Chimbuk, Meghla, Shoilo Propat for adventure lovers.Bandarban is an administrative region of the Chittagong Division in the southeastern part of Bangladesh. It belongs to the Chittagong Hill Tracts region. The distance from Chittagong to Bandarban district is 75 kilometers. Bandarban district has become an important tourist destination in Bangladesh for its natural diversity.
